Usage Rules
DOUse Agdasima for all headings, KPI numbers, wordmarks, and large display text across PhoennixAI products.
DOUse slate (#556970) as the primary interactive colour — active nav items, buttons, focus rings, and links.
DOUse olive (#ABA944) as a sparingly used accent — badges, active states, highlights, and the 'ix' in the wordmark.
DOKeep all PhoennixAI product interfaces light mode (#F7F6F2 background, #FFFFFF surfaces).
DORender the PhoennixAI logo always on its dark background (#0D1520) as a circular mark. Do not place it on light backgrounds without the dark circle.
DON'TUse purple (#5B5FEE, #6B4FBB, #8B5CF6) in any PhoennixAI product or document. Purple belongs to Phantom Gaming Studio.
DON'TUse Inter or JetBrains Mono for PhoennixAI products — those are deprecated. Use Agdasima / Source Serif 4 / Fira Code only.
DON'TMix the VANTAGE navy/amber brand into PhoennixAI interfaces. VANTAGE has its own separate design system.
DON'TUse Clash Display — that was the pre-2026 brand typography. It is retired. Agdasima is the current display font.
